CANFAR Infrastructure Team

The CANFAR Infrastructure Team is responsible for:

  • Designing and developing various components of the CANFAR infrastructure
  • Evaluating and recommending existing software packages to be included in the CANFAR infrastructure
  • Modifying existing software packages to support the CANFAR infrastructure
  • Reviewing the designs and code produced by peers
  • Improving the network throughput among the CANFAR infrastructure sites
  • Working with network administrators at each CANFAR partner site, CANARIE, and ORANs to establish common monitoring infrastructure
  • Design, implement and test a processing system on a testbed cluster
  • Move the test system onto production clusters

The CANFAR Infrastructure Team is composed of software and network specialists located at the University of Victoria's High Energy Physics Group and at the National Research Council's Canadian Astronomy Data Centre.

How to use CANFAR

User Guides, Tutorials, Toolboxes and and general help can be found on the CANFAR Documentation Wiki. Help on getting a CANFAR account is available from the wiki or by contacting canfarhelp@nrc.gc.ca.
